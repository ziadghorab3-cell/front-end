About WE School

WE School is dedicated to helping students learn web development with simple, hands-on lessons and projects. Our goal is to make learning HTML, CSS, and responsive design fun and easy for beginners.

We believe that practice makes perfect, so every course includes real projects where students apply what they’ve learned.
